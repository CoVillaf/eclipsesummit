About the Conference

The Eclipse Ecosystem is a LOT MORE than just the Eclipse IDE. Its hosts 340+ projects: MQTT, Jetty, Hudson, Vert.x, AspectJ, GeoMesa, StatET, RCP/SWT, Paho, Babel, EGerrit and many more...

Eclipse Summit India 2017 is the 2nd annual conference presented by the local Eclipse Community in collaboration with The Eclipse Foundation. It is designed by the community, for the community. This is a great opportunity for the Eclipse fraternity in India to learn, explore, share, and collaborate on the latest ideas and information about Eclipse ecosystem and its community of projects with the core contributors, adopters, extenders, service providers, consumers and business and research organizations.

Day 1 of the conference is dedicated to Open IoT (Internet of Things).
Day 2 and 3 will look at other Eclipse Projects and case studies.

Want to Present @ Eclipse Summit India?

To present at Eclipse Summit India 2017, please submit your proposal before 28th April 2017 via our public proposal submission system.

Unlike many other conferences, the sessions and keynotes at Eclipse Summit India are chosen by an independent program committee, allowing us to consistently offer a high-quality program. To contact the program committee, send us an email.

The themes for talks at Eclipse Summit India include the most interesting current topics. The 2017 conference program will include both hands-on tutorials and technical sessions on current topics pertinent to the Eclipse developer community, such as Eclipse Platform/RCP/Runtimes, Languages and Tools, Web/Mobile/Cloud Development, Modeling, Methodology and Devops, Embedded, IoT, Science, Java 9, IDEs and more, as well as sessions that demonstrate Eclipse-based solutions for finance, automotive, aerospace, insurance, and other domains. The exact themes are listed on the CFP site.
